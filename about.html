[Miscellania]

Under the Smoke is a student project website designed as a tribute for the poet and author Carl Sandburg. This website uses public domain photographs of early 20th century Chicago in combination with the poetry of Carl Sandburg. The purpose of this website is to educate on a great American poet and the city he wrote of.
